F.A.Q's

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a brand strategy consultancy, and how is it different from marketing or advertising?

A brand strategy consultancy focuses on defining the core elements that shape your brand’s identity—such as your brand persona, unique value propositions, and positioning within the market. This lays the groundwork for all future marketing and advertising efforts. In short, we help you build the foundation that marketing builds upon.

How does your brand strategy service improve customer insistence and conversions?

We use a combination of in-depth market research, competitor analysis, and real customer insights to identify what truly resonates with your audience. With those insights we design brand persona’s and offer’s designed to meet both the emotional and functional needs of your target audience, ensuring they choose your brand time and again.

How long does it take to create a brand strategy?

Typically, our process takes about 21 days from reviewing your existing brand or vision to delivery. This timeline ensures we have ample time to conduct market research, develop your brand persona, and craft an offer that stands out. However, the timeline may be adjusted depending on the complexity of your needs and how quickly you can provide input at various stages.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we work across a broad range of industries, our expertise lies in sectors where brand differentiation and customer loyalty are critical. We have successfully partnered with clients in technology, consumer goods, healthcare, and professional services, among others. Our approach is tailored to the unique challenges of each industry, ensuring that the strategies we create are highly relevant.

What exactly do you include in your market research?

Our market research is comprehensive and action-oriented. We don’t just gather data—we dig deep into customer behaviors, preferences, pain points, and decision-making processes. This allows us to identify the gaps in the market and uncover insights that shape both your brand strategy and offer. We also analyze competitor strategies to ensure that your brand stands out where it matters most.

How is your brand strategy service different from others?

Our approach is entirely customized. Rather than offering cookie-cutter solutions, we tailor every aspect of the strategy based on real customer insights, industry trends, and competitive analysis. We combine this data-driven approach with creativity to deliver a strategy that’s not only unique but also highly actionable.

What do you mean by a "mouth-watering offer"?

A mouth-watering offer is one that your target audience finds irresistible. It's a combination of pricing, product/service features, and unique benefits that speak directly to your customers’ needs and desires. We love to make it difficult for prospects to say "no."

What is the McKinsey MECE framework, and how does it improve reporting?

The MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) framework is a method we use to present our findings and recommendations in a clear, organized manner. It ensures that every part of our strategy is both distinct and comprehensive, leaving no gaps. It also makes it easier for you to understand the data and act on our recommendations.

How do I know if your strategies will work for my business?

Our strategies are based on rigorous research and tailored to your unique business context. While each client is different, we always start with data: understanding your audience, analyzing competitors, and identifying gaps in the market. This ensures that the strategy is designed to meet the specific needs of your business, and not just based on general principles.

How involved do I need to be in the strategy creation process?

Your input is crucial, especially in the discovery phase where we seek to understand your business, brand, and goals. After that, our team takes over the heavy lifting, presenting you with detailed research and strategy drafts at key milestones. You can be as hands-on or hands-off as you’d like, but we always keep communication open to ensure the strategy aligns with your vision.

What happens after I receive my brand strategy?

Once we deliver your brand strategy, we don’t just leave you with a document. We walk you through the entire strategy, explaining each element and how it applies to your business.

What’s the next step after I fill out the contact form?

After filling out the contact form, we’ll schedule an initial consultation call to discuss your business, your goals, and how we can help. During this call, we’ll walk you through our process, answer any questions you have, and outline the next steps for getting started.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing starts from $1000 and varies based on your desired deliverables and the depth of engagement.  You can choose the exact combination of services you need whether it's market research—where we uncover customer segments, competitor analysis, and industry trends; brand strategy—where we define your brand's core identity, align it with your target audience, and craft a unique value proposition; or offer development—where we design offers based on real customer insights to maximize conversions. 

Is your brand strategy service a one-time project or ongoing support?

While the core brand strategy is delivered as a one-time project, we offer options for ongoing support and consultation. If you need help implementing the strategy or refining it over time, we’re available to continue working with you as your brand grows and evolves.
